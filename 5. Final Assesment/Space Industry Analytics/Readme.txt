Space Industry Analytics
Who's Winning the New Space Race?


About Dataset
A comprehensive dataset comparing three major space organizations
SpaceX, NASA, and Rocket Lab across 15 years (2010-2024).

Files Included:

Space_Industry_Analytics_2010_2024.csv → All 3 companies combined (45 rows)
SpaceX_Analytics_2010_2024.csv → SpaceX only (15 rows)
NASA_Analytics_2010_2024.csv → NASA only (15 rows)
RocketLab_Analytics_2010_2024.csv → Rocket Lab only (15 rows)
Columns:
Company, Year, Launches, Successful_Launches, Failed_Launches,
Success_Rate_%, Revenue_USD_M, Budget_Funding_USD_M, Employees, Rockets

Data Sources:

SpaceX: Wikipedia launch records + Reuters/Payload revenue reports
NASA: nasa.gov official annual budget reports
Rocket Lab: SEC 10-K filings (publicly traded company)
