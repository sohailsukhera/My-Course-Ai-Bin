The 2026 Tech Mega-IPO Wave: SpaceX, OpenAI & More
About Dataset
The largest IPO pipeline in history hit the public markets in 2026 — anchored by SpaceX's record-shattering June debut (the biggest offering ever) and followed by OpenAI, Anthropic, Stripe, Databricks and a dozen more unicorns worth a combined ~$3 trillion. This dataset is a clean, curated reference of the marquee 2026 tech IPOs: valuations, capital raised, revenue/ARR, and derived price-to-revenue multiples for comparing how the market is pricing the AI era.

Figures are hand-compiled from public reporting (CNBC, CNN Business, Fortune, Yahoo Finance, Built In, Dealroom, Forge Global, Benzinga) as of mid-June 2026. Several valuations are estimates or targets for offerings not yet priced, and revenue figures mix audited revenue with company-reported ARR (see revenue_type). The valuation_to_revenue_multiple and years_to_ipo columns are derived/computed. Treat forward-looking valuations as estimates, not measured facts or investment advice.

Suggested analyses: AI vs non-AI valuation multiples, private vs public pricing (up-rounds/down-rounds), bubble charts of valuation vs revenue, time-to-liquidity by sector, and AI's share of total pipeline valuation.
